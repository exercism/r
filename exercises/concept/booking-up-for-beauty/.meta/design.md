# Design

## Goal

The goal of this exercise is to introduce the student to dates and datetimes in R, and especially the lubridate library.

## Learning objectives

- Understand Base R versus lubridate functions.
- Understand the underlying `date` and `datetime` types.
- Understand date(time) construction, from parsing strings or from numerical values.
- Understand string formatting and some of the gotchas.
- Understand `period`, `duration` and `interval` types, and how they differ.
- Understand that there are many other useful utility functions, available to people who ***read the manual***.

## Out of scope

- Most locale issues, other than forcing UTC for `now()`.

## Concepts

The Concepts this exercise unlocks are:

- `dates-times`
